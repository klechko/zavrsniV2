Photo Equipment

  • Big Minolta
  • Minolta X-300s
  • Fuji DL-25
  • Olympus E-PL1

Minolta Dynax 700si

My first DSLR camera. I use it with Tamron 28-200mm lens. Huge, but awesome

Minolta X-300s

I bought this camera just for the 50mm MD Rokkor lens, which is spectacular. It's best used for portraits or miniatures considering it's huge aperture opening (f1.7). Sadly, this camera is getting ready for retirement.

Fuji DL-25

I got this one from my parents. They used it for trips, family gatherings and construction supervision. We called it an "idiot" - no settings whatsoever, just point and shoot.

Olympus E-PL1

Old mirrorless camera. It's got a lot of options, decent CMOS sensor and a cheap lens. Considering I rarely use it, it's an OK device.
